Full Itinerary

Designed for Skaria Tours and Travels using the provided tour content.

DAY 01

Paris, France

The Eiffel Tower

Begin the tour with Paris’s most iconic monument. The Eiffel Tower gives guests a grand first impression of the city, with photo opportunities, elegant surroundings and unforgettable views of the Paris skyline.

Arc de Triomphe

Visit one of the strongest symbols of French history and national pride. Located at the top of the Champs-Élysées, this monument is admired for its sculpted details, ceremonial importance and the Tomb of the Unknown Soldier beneath it.

DAY 02

Paris City Tour

Place de la Concorde

Explore one of Paris’s most important public squares, positioned between the Champs-Élysées and the Tuileries Garden. The wide open setting, fountains and historic atmosphere make it a memorable stop on the city tour.

Notre Dame Cathedral

Admire one of Paris’s treasured Gothic landmarks, famous for its towers, rose windows, stone carvings and sacred heritage. The cathedral area also offers a beautiful riverside atmosphere on the Île de la Cité.

Sacré-Cœur Basilica

Visit the white-domed Basilica of the Sacred Heart on Montmartre hill. The location is known for peaceful prayer, artistic streets nearby and lovely elevated views over Paris.

The Louvre Museum

Discover one of the world’s greatest museums, recognised for its glass pyramid and extraordinary collections of art, sculpture and historical treasures. It is a major cultural highlight of the Paris program.

Seine River Cruise

Enjoy a relaxing Bateaux Mouches cruise through the heart of Paris. The route gives guests a different view of bridges, riverside buildings and famous monuments, especially beautiful during the evening.

DAY 03

Disneyland Paris

Full Day Disneyland

Spend a joyful full day at Disneyland Paris, one of Europe’s most loved family attractions. Guests can enjoy themed lands, entertainment, character moments, rides, parades and a colourful holiday atmosphere.

Overnight in Disney Paris

After the park experience, relax and enjoy the lively Disney surroundings. The overnight stay gives travelers time to unwind after a fun-filled day before continuing the journey towards Germany.

DAY 04

Titisee, Germany

Titisee Lake

Visit a peaceful lake destination in Germany’s Black Forest region. Titisee is known for calm water views, lakeside walks, souvenir shops and a refreshing natural setting ideal for a short leisure stop.

Cuckoo Clock Factory

Experience the traditional craft of Black Forest cuckoo clocks. This stop introduces guests to local workmanship, carved wooden designs and one of Germany’s most recognisable cultural souvenirs.

Black Forest

Enjoy the charm of Germany’s famous forest region, with dense greenery, rolling landscapes, small villages and a calm countryside feel. It is also associated with cuckoo clocks, spa towns and Black Forest cake.

DAY 05

Zurich, Switzerland

Schaffhausen Rhine Falls

See one of Europe’s most powerful waterfalls near Schaffhausen. The roaring water, viewing platforms and dramatic natural setting make Rhine Falls one of Switzerland’s most impressive scenic attractions.

Lindt Home of Chocolate

Enjoy Switzerland’s famous chocolate culture through an interactive chocolate experience and shop. It is a sweet stop for families and groups, especially for those interested in Swiss food traditions and gifts.

DAY 06

Lucerne, Switzerland

Lake Lucerne

Spend time around one of Switzerland’s most beautiful lake regions, surrounded by mountains, clean streets and peaceful views. Lucerne offers a classic Swiss atmosphere with old town charm and scenic photo points.

Chapel Bridge

See Lucerne’s famous covered wooden bridge across the River Reuss. The bridge and nearby Water Tower create one of the city’s most photographed views and reflect the historic charm of old Lucerne.

The Lion Monument

Visit the emotional rock-carved Lion Monument, created in memory of the Swiss Guards who died during the French Revolution. Its quiet surroundings make it a meaningful historical stop.

DAY 07

Interlaken, Switzerland

Lauterbrunnen

Travel through one of Switzerland’s most scenic alpine valleys, famous for steep cliffs, green meadows and waterfall views. It is a beautiful nature stop and a favourite location for photography.

Trummelbach Falls

Visit glacier-fed waterfalls hidden inside the mountain in the Lauterbrunnen Valley. Walkways and tunnels allow guests to experience the force of nature from close viewpoints.

Harder Kulm

Reach the “Top of Interlaken” viewpoint for panoramic scenery over Lake Brienz, Lake Thun and the Swiss Alps. It is one of the strongest visual highlights of the Interlaken day.

Paragliding

Optional paragliding gives adventure lovers a memorable aerial view of Interlaken, lakes and alpine landscapes. It can be arranged based on weather, time availability and guest preference.

DAY 08

Milan, Italy

Milan Departure

Complete the 7N/8D Europe journey with memories of Paris, Disney, Germany and Switzerland. Depending on flight timing, guests may enjoy a relaxed transfer and final photo stops before departure.

Airport Transfer

Proceed comfortably to the airport for the return journey. The tour concludes with assistance for transfer arrangements and farewell from the travel team.
